This guide covers the process for submitting Bruin Alpha Investment at UCLA to the official UCLA Undergrad Business Society (UBS) Club Directory.

## 4. Club Description (~150 words)
Use this tightened, UCLA-approved mission summary:

```text
Bruin Alpha Investment at UCLA is a student-led organization dedicated to bridging academic finance with real-world market application. We bring together students interested in asset and wealth management, financial analysis, sales & trading, and prediction market modeling. Members actively participate in real trading environments, applying arbitrage strategies and advanced technical techniques to live markets. 

The organization emphasizes practical execution, disciplined risk management, and a deep understanding of global market structure. Through a collaborative, performance-driven community, we equip our members with the technical skills and strategic thinking required to succeed in competitive finance careers. Bruin Alpha Investment acts independently of UCLA and does not represent the University.
```

- [ ] **Verification**: Once approved, search the [UBS Directory](https://uclaubs.com/club-directory) to ensure the listing appears correctly.
