# Instagram Setup Guide

This guide covers the manual setup of the official Instagram account for Bruin Alpha Investment at UCLA.

## 1. Account Creation
- [ ] Sign up at [Instagram.com](https://www.instagram.com/).
- [ ] **Email**: `bruinalphainvestment26@gmail.com`
- [ ] **Password**: Use a strong, unique password (store in shared Bitwarden).
- [ ] **2FA**: Enable Two-Factor Authentication immediately using a shared authenticator or Mack's phone (noted in `docs/HANDOFF.md`).

## 2. Profile Setup
- [ ] **Username**: `@bruinalphainvestment` (Alternative: `@bai.ucla`)
- [ ] **Display Name**: `Bruin Alpha Investment at UCLA`
- [ ] **Bio**:
  ```text
  Have Passion, Believe in Legacy, Believe in BAI.
  Bridging academic finance with real-world market application.
  👇 Link to Website
  [Website URL]
  ```
- [ ] **Profile Photo**: Upload the BAI logo, cropped tightly to a square.
- [ ] **Website**: Link to the live site.

## 3. Professional Account
- [ ] Go to **Settings** > **Account Type**.
- [ ] Switch to a **Professional Account**.
- [ ] Category: `Education` OR `Investment Management`.

## 4. Content Strategy (Phase 1)
- [ ] **Post 1 (Mission)**: A high-quality graphic of the logo with the mission statement in the caption.
- [ ] **Post 2 (Founders)**: A carousel introducing the founding team.
- [ ] **Timing**: Defer active posting until 2-3 weeks before the Enormous Activities Fair (EAF).
- [ ] **Engagement**: Do NOT mass-follow accounts until the EAF recruitment push.

## 5. Recording the URL
- [ ] Copy the final Profile URL.
- [ ] Paste into Sanity Studio: `SiteSettings` > `Instagram URL`.
- [ ] Record the URL in `docs/HANDOFF.md`.
